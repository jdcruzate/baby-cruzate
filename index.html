Our Story

Sixteen years. That's how long we waited, grew, and prepared — not just in practical ways, but in every way that truly matters. Sixteen years of building a life together, of learning who we are, of becoming the people we always hoped our child would look up to.

For all of those sixteen years, we poured every ounce of our love into our firstborn, Seltiel Jaime. He has been our whole heart — our greatest joy, our proudest achievement, our reason to be better every single day. Loving only him, fully and completely, didn't shrink our hearts. It stretched them. It made them bigger, deeper, and more ready than we ever thought possible.

And now, finally, we are ready to love again. A second baby boy — just as prayed for, just as dreamed about, and just as deeply wanted as his Kuya before him.

This little one wasn't just planned. He was waited for, believed in, and worth every single moment. We believe that God's timing is always perfect, and this season is beautiful proof of that.

We'd be so glad to have you join us in this journey — as we count down the days, prepare our hearts and home, and get ready to welcome our greatest new adventure. Thank you for being part of our story. 💙
